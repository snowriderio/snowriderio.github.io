Play Endless Winter Racing Online

Snow Rush 3D is an endless winter racing game where players control a sled or snowboard sliding down snowy mountains. The game focuses on reflex-based movement, increasing speed, and distance-based scoring. It supports both single-player and two-player modes. Built with 3D environments and automatic downhill movement. The game challenges players to react quickly to changing terrain, ramps, and hazards.

Scoring, Gifts, and Progression

Snow Rush 3D uses a distance-centered progression system.

Distance Scoring

  • Every meter increases score.
  • No bonus multipliers.
  • Ranking depends on survival time.
  • Distance is the primary success metric.

Gift Box System

Gift boxes serve as in-game currency. They are used for:

  • Unlocking new sled designs
  • Accessing visual customization
  • Progress tracking

Collecting gifts often requires leaving optimal paths, adding risk.
