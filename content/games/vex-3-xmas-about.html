Vex 3 Xmas Platform Game – Play Winter Stickman Adventure Online

Vex 3 Xmas is a winter-themed platform adventure game based on the classic Vex series. Players control a stickman character across snowy levels filled with traps, moving platforms, and timing-based obstacles. The game focuses on precision movement, fast reactions, and repeated level mastery. Set in a Christmas-themed environment, Vex 3 Xmas keeps the core mechanics of the original Vex while adding seasonal visuals, music, and themed hazards.

Players face spikes, saws, icicles, lasers, falling platforms, and crushing blocks, each demanding exact timing, controlled movement, and pattern recognition. Late-game levels combine multiple hazards in tight spaces, pushing players toward near-perfect execution.
