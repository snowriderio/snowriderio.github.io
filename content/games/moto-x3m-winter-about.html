What Is Moto X3M Winter?

Moto X3M Winter is the winter version of the popular Moto X3M bike racing series created by MadPuffers. Released in December 2017, the game features brand-new snowy tracks and festive environments. Players must guide their motorbike across icy bridges, ramps, and traps while trying to finish each level as quickly as possible.

Tips to Improve Your Performance

If you want to earn three stars on every track, follow these tips:

Beginner Tips

  • Do not hold the accelerator all the time
  • Brake before dangerous jumps
  • Keep your bike balanced in the air

Advanced Strategies

  • Start flipping before reaching ramps
  • Memorize trap locations
  • Control rotation during long jumps
  • Avoid risky stunts near the finish line
